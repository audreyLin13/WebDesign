Taiwan's Must-Eats:

Beef Noodle Soup (牛肉面)

📖 Also Known As: N/A
🍅 Main Ingredients: beef shank, spicy bean paste, soy sauce, cilantro, Chinese aromatic herbs, and mustard greens
🏬 Best Place to Get Them: Lin Dong Fang Beef Noodles (林東芳牛肉麵)

Stinky Tofu (臭豆腐)

📖 Also Known As:Changsha Chou Doufu
🍅 Main Ingredients: shrimp paste, firm tofu, soft tofu, and Chinese herbs
🏬 Best Place to Get Them: Raohe Street Night Market (饒河街觀光夜市)

Craving Something Savory?

Oyster Omelette (蚵仔煎)

📖 Also Known As:Taiwanese Oyster Pancake
🍅 Main Ingredients: Chinese greens, fresh oysters, soy sauce and red miso paste
🏬 Best Place to Get Them: Yuan Huan Pien (圓環邊)

Gua Bao (割包)

📖 Also Known As: Taiwanese Pork Belly Buns
🍅 Main Ingredients: steamed buns, braised pork belly, pickled mustard greens, fresh cilantro, and peanut powder
🏬 Best Place to Get Them: Lan Jia Gua Bao (藍家割包)

Hujiao Bing (胡椒餅)

📖 Also Known As:Taiwanese Pepper Pork Buns
🍅 Main Ingredients: Sesame buns, ground pork, sesame oil, black pepper, and garlic
🏬 Best Place to Get Them:Fuzhou Shizu Pepper Buns (福州胡椒餅)

Scallion Beef Rolls (牛肉捲餅)

📖 Also Known As: Green Onion Pancake Beef Rolls
🍅 Main Ingredients: Slow-cooked beef (typically beef shank), cucumbers, cilantro, hoisin sauce, scallions, and sesame oil.
🏬 Best Place to Get Them: Yong He Soy Milk King (永和豆漿大王)

Craving Something Sweet?

Boba Tea (波霸奶茶)

📖 Also Known As: Tapioca Milk Tea or Bubble Tea
🍅 Main Ingredients: tapioca starch, tea, milk, and toppings of your choosing
🏬 Best Place to Get Them: Chun Shui Tang (春水堂)

Pineapple Cakes (鳳梨酥)

📖 Also Known As: N/A
🍅 Main Ingredients: powdered milk, pineapple jam or fresh pineapple and pastry flour
🏬 Best Place to Get Them: Chia Te Taipei (佳德鳳梨酥)
