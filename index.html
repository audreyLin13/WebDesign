taiwan 101:

COVID-19 policy:

Do I have to quarantine?
Yes. Taiwan requires all travelers to undergo a mandatory 3-day quarantine.
Which vaccines are available in Taiwan?
AstraZeneca, Moderna, BNT and Medigen.
Do I need a negative test to get into Taiwan?
Each traveler must complete four rapid tests that will be provided to them at an international airport.
When should I take my rapid test?
Perform rapid tests at the following times:
1. Day of arrival
2. 2 days prior to when you first go out
3. Last day of quarantine (3rd day)
4. If you begin to experience any symptoms (i.e. sore throat, nausea, fever, and more)
