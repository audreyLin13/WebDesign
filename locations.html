For the Eager Tourist...

Taipei 101

Located in the heart of Taiwan's capital, Taipei, Taipei 101 is a staple tourist spot for anyone. Visitors can travel up the elevator to get vast views of the whole city AND surrounding mountains!

Built in 1952, the Grand Hotel is 14-floor tall hotel, overlooking the extensive Keelung River. With vast, palace-like architecture, the hotel attracts thousands of

One of the nine beautiful and diverse national parks in Taiwan, Yangmingshan is full of luscious green scenery and various trails, scenic spots, hot springs and more.
